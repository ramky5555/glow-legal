Account Deletion — Smiro

We make it easy to leave. Your data, your choice.

If you'd like to permanently delete your Smiro account and all associated data, you can do so directly inside the app or by contacting us by email.

Deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted, your session history, awareness scores, and profile data cannot be recovered.

Option 1 — Delete in the app

The fastest way to delete your account:

  1. Open Smiro and sign in.
  2. Tap Profile (bottom right).
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted.

Your account and all data are deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you can't access the app, email us and we will delete your account within 7 business days.

  • Email: ramky555@gmail.com
  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • Include the email address associated with your Smiro account.

What gets deleted

  • Your name and email address (stored in AWS Cognito).
  • All session history and awareness scores (stored in AWS DynamoDB).
  • All account preferences and settings.

We do not store camera images or facial data — these are processed on-device only and are never transmitted to our servers.
